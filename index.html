    Avoiding Stimulants

    Stimulants are substances that increase or stimulate, the normal activity of central nervous system

    In other words, they increase the normal level of awareness, alertness and energy in my mind and body

    They usually have side-effects and if i take them for an extended period of time i may become dependent upon them

    There are legal and illegal Stimulants

    Legal stimulants like caffeine, nicotine, weight-loss pills and energy drinks are very popular

    However, they are aloso very addictive

    Once i started using them, i nay find that when i am not drinking my coffee or smoking my cigarette, i cannot stop thinking about it

    When i make a decision to quit more often than not, i will have some mild withdrawal symptoms such as irritability or procupation with the drink or cigarette

    Stimulants, like amphetamine, which are used to treat medical conditions, are considered a 'controlled substance '( i need a prescription from a doctor) due to the extremely high chances of becamibg addicted or dependent upon the drug

    Illegal stimulants such as methamphetamine, crack , cocaine or ecstasy are extremely addictive

    While under influence of these controlled substances i may become everly sexual active and often times hsve unprotected sex

    I may also find myself involved in criminal activities in an attempt to support my habit

    Possible effects of misusing stimulants are taking a lot too much activity being annoyed easily, nervousness, dilated pupils , high blood pressure, long periods of not sleeping and not feeling hungry

    We use stimulants to make hs feel good. If i try to control a symptoms with stimulants, the cause of that symptom will remain. So i will try to stay away from stimulantsas much as i can
